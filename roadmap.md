# Development Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines a **realistic, multi-year timeline** for building sustainable football infrastructure and a functioning token economy.

We prioritize **execution over hype** and **substance over speed**.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Fundraising

**Timeline:** Q1-Q2 2026

### Objectives
- Complete whitepaper and project documentation
- Conduct token sale rounds (Seed, Private, Public)
- Establish legal structure (SAD formation)
- Secure initial partnerships with clubs

### Milestones
- [x] Whitepaper published
- [x] Core team assembled
- [ ] Seed round completed (USD 850k @ $0.05)
- [ ] Private round completed (USD 2M @ $0.10)
- [ ] Public / Launchpad round completed (USD 2.25M @ $0.15)
- [ ] Smart contracts deployed and audited
- [ ] Initial DEX liquidity established (USD 600k)

### Success Criteria
- ~USD 5M raised
- Token launched and trading
- Legal entity operational

## Phase 2: Infrastructure Construction

**Timeline:** Q3 2026 - Q2 2027

### Objectives
- Build the sports city facility
- Hire technical and operational staff
- Establish club partnerships
- Begin player scouting and recruitment

### Milestones
- [ ] Land development and site preparation
- [ ] Field construction (natural and artificial turf)
- [ ] Performance center and gym construction
- [ ] Locker rooms and support facilities
- [ ] Technology and equipment installation
- [ ] Staff hiring (coaches, analysts, operations)

- [ ] Milestone token burn: construction complete (2M tokens)

- Facility construction completed
- Full operational team in place
- Active partnerships established

---

## Key Assumptions

### Conservative Approach

This roadmap is built on **conservative assumptions**:

- Construction may take longer than planned
- Player development requires 2-3 years minimum
- Transfer market timing is unpredictable
- Revenue ramp-up is gradual

### Flexibility

We maintain flexibility to:

- Accelerate if opportunities arise
- Adjust to market conditions
- Pivot based on learnings
- Respond to community feedback

- Financial transactions and treasury balance
